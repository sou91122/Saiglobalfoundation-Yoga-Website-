about us

The Sai Humanitarian Institute of Learning & Healing /dba Sai Foundation, a Tax-exempt Non-profit 501(c)(3) Organization, California, was founded by Mira Sai Ma in 2001 under the guidance of Sri Sai Baba. She was guided into a divine vision and mission to provide a Safe, Sacred, Divine platform for seekers of all persuasions, by establishing a Multi-faith Holistic Temple Sanctuary.

The current holistic sanctuary once established, would welcome all those seeking self-realization and answers to the what and why of life while also wishing to discover the deeper purpose of their life journey.

A Wish-fulfilling Temple sanctuary like no other!

THE KALPATARU SAI SANCTUARY

Here one will feel at home with the Divine Presence inside and outside one self. Designed and equipped to transport one from the mind space of worries and problems to a place of calm and peace, one can come here and feel assured that one's prayers will be answered.
